Hi there, my name is Tyson Zevenbergen,
and I am an engineer & user researcher

I am an electrical engineer that has been working in industrial manufacturing for the past 15 years. My day job is a OEM Technical Consultant for Rockwell Automation. I help machine builders in the Pacific Northwest learn the proper application of Rockwell Automation hardware and software. The primary industries in this region are aerospace, agriculture, packaging and timber products.

My goal is the Digital Fabrication calss is to learn the methods and tools used in rapid prototyping. I plan to quickly begin using the skills developed in this class to begin contribute designs to the automation industry. We are within the technology knee of the robotics and automation field and I am excited to be learning the skills to acclerate the number and quality of products those in my field have at their disposal.
