Proposed Concept

The concept for this design is helping children learn to write their alphabet. The design is three chalkboard tablets (Step 1, Step 2, and Step 3). Step 1 for the beginner has the alphabet indented in the chalkboard to guide writting and a manuscript with detail the sequence of lines for each letter. Step 2 has the manuscript to remind children the proper sequence of creating each letter. Step 3 will only have the lines etched on the chalkboard.

To limit risk I will pursue two methods for the Step 1 tablet which will be the most complex to create. The first strategy, "Slate Stategy", is the media blast the slate with a vinyl cover over the slate to protect all areas of the slate except the letters. The second strategy, "Milled Board Strategy" , is a piece of 1" x 6" board that will be covered with chalk uilt a can of chalkboard paint and a laser engraving the manuscript over the chalkboard paint.

Breakdown of Tasks

This design will be implemented using the following methods:
1. CAD MODELING (Rhino) to creating a parameterized cutout of the alphabet on the chalkboard
2. MILLING will be used create the alphabet indented on the Step 1 design.
3. 3D PRINTER will to use to create a chalk holder
4. LASER ETCHING to overlay proper alphabet manuscript on the chalkboard.
